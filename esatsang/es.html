e-Satsang Transmission Schedule

 April 2017 - May 2017

 

1. Timings are subject to change, and changes if any, will be notified in the Table below. Please therefore keep checking this web site for updated information.

2. Please note the station practice schedule given at the end of this page.

3. All e-Satsang Teams must be ready to receive transmission at least 15 minutes before the Station Connect Time indicated in the Table below.
4. No station is permitted to connect for any transmission unless five initiated Satsangis are present at the time of connection and during the entire duration of any transmission.
5. With effect from 26.12.2010, stations attending Bhandara at Dayalbagh are permitted to receive e-Satsang Transmission on ARTI/BHANDARA provided there is at least one e-SATSANG Team Member of the Branch to manage the Password and operate the systems, AND there are at least five initiated Satsangis present for the transmission (see Point 4 above). The Arti/Bhandara Transmission will not take place to stations that have been permitted only Weekly e-Satsang.
